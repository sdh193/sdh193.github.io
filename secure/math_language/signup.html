There is some good advice and rules about making a good username and password. Here it is:
  • Make sure your username is less than 10 characters. Usernames are best when they are easy to remember.
  • Your password should be more than 8 characters and less than 15 characters.
  • Your password is stored in a highly secured place where no hacker can hijack it unless they get access to your PC.
